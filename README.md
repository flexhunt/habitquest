## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### What is HabitQuest?
HabitQuest is a free, open-source habit tracking app that uses gamification to help you build better habits. It combines habit tracking with RPG-style progression, social features, and journaling.

### Is HabitQuest free?
Yes! HabitQuest is completely free and open-source. There are no hidden costs or premium features behind a paywall.

### Does HabitQuest work offline?
Yes, HabitQuest caches your data locally and syncs when you're back online. You can track habits, view your streaks, and read journals offline.

### Can I use HabitQuest with friends?
Absolutely! Create or join a squad to battle bosses together, chat in real-time, and climb the leaderboard as a team.

### What platforms does HabitQuest support?
HabitQuest is available as a Progressive Web App (works on any browser) and as a native Android app. iOS support is planned.

### How does the XP system work?
Every habit you complete earns you XP. The amount depends on the habit's difficulty, your current streak, and bonus multipliers. Level up to unlock new achievements!

### Can I import my habits from other apps?
Currently, manual import is supported via settings. We're working on direct integrations with popular habit trackers.
